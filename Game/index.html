⚙️ Cogfall

A clockwork deckbuilding roguelike. The Aurum Core — a god-sized machine — died long ago, and now something is winding it back to life. You are a reactivated automaton, fighting up through its rusted sectors with a deck of cards, salvaging new gear and Cogs, to reach and shatter its heart. Every run reshapes the machine — choose your path wisely.

Play a card by clicking it (attacks ask you to pick a target). Spend your Steam each turn, then End Turn. Plating (your block) absorbs damage. Each machine plays differently — read your fighter's signature below.

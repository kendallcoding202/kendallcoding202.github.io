Spire of Trials

A deckbuilding roguelike in the spirit of Slay the Spire. Climb the spire floor by floor. Fight monsters with a deck of cards, grow stronger with new cards and relics, and defeat the boss that waits at the top. Every run is different — choose your path wisely.

Play a card by clicking it (attacks ask you to pick a target). Spend your energy each turn, then End Turn. Block resets every turn, so use it when you need it.

## Long "About This Game"
*(Supports Steam BBCode: [h2], [b], [list], [img], etc.)*

[h2]YOU ARE THE INTRUSION[/h2]
BREACH is a roguelike deckbuilder about breaking into systems that don't want you there. Your deck is your toolkit — port scans, zero-days, logic bombs, proxy chains, cover-tracks. Each layer of a target is a puzzle: reveal what's guarding it, pick the right exploit for the right defense, and stay under the detection ceiling. Play loud and fast, or slip in silent and leave no log. The system is watching the whole time.

[h2]EVERY RUN IS A DIFFERENT BREAK-IN[/h2]
Runs play out on a branching map, Slay-the-Spire style — every route commits you to a different way through. Scout ahead, weigh the loud-but-lucrative job against the quiet detour, and manage the Trace: a run-wide heat meter that makes the whole network harder the more noise you make.
[list]
[*][b]Four operators[/b], each a different way to break in — a silent ghost, a brute-force battering ram, a logic-bomb saboteur, and a combo-chaining speedrunner. Distinct starting decks and signature passives.
[*][b]Per-run system modifiers[/b] reroll every job so the same target never plays the same twice.
[*][b]An event deck[/b] of contacts, favors, and traps between jobs.
[*][b]Implants[/b] — persistent cyberware you collect mid-run that reshapes your whole build.
[*][b]Short, medium, and long campaigns[/b] — a 20-minute smash-and-grab or a full heist.
[/list]

[h2]THE SYSTEM WATCHES BACK[/h2]
Every campaign has a watcher — a rogue AI or a corp security handler that reads your position on the map and transmits directly into your terminal as you close in. It gets colder, sharper, and more personal the deeper you go. And it's not just flavor: let the Trace climb and the watcher warns your targets ahead of you — systems start alert, reinforced, and patching faster. The hunt is a mechanic.

[h2]BUILD AROUND A STYLE[/h2]
Cards carry synergy tags — Ghost, Overload, Worm, Chain — and each archetype has payoff keystones that scale the harder you commit. Go full-silent and turn stealth into damage. Stack logic bombs and detonate a whole network at once. Chain a dozen plays in a single turn. The reward pool lets you draft toward the build the run is handing you.

[h2]META-PROGRESSION[/h2]
Clear a campaign to raise your Threat Level — escalating ascension-style difficulty that stacks new pressure each tier — and grow your Operator Profile across runs, unlocking harder contracts as you prove you can get out clean.

[h2]FEATURES[/h2]
[list]
[*]Deckbuilding roguelike with a full intrusion theme — no filler, every card is a tactic
[*]Branching campaign map with meaningful routes and a run-wide heat system
[*]4 playable operators with distinct decks and passives
[*]A reactive antagonist that hunts you mechanically, not just narratively
[*]Per-run modifiers, an event deck, and collectible implants for high replayability
[*]Threat Levels + Operator Profile meta-progression
[*]Slick terminal aesthetic, fully synthesized sound, deterministic seeded runs
[/list]
